Our Philosophy

There are over 20 ironSource platforms running, and they all share one thing — each was built from scratch. That means we’ve collectively put in more hours rebuilding the same drop down component than it takes to write ‘hello world’ in Brainfuck.

That's where FusionUI comes in. This framework is ironSource’s first inner-source project. Built from the ground up, by ironSorcerers, for ironSorcerers, we hope that this project inspires collaboration and innovation.

Building Solutions

FusionUI is a plug & play dashboard solution, letting you create beautiful dashboards that answer real business needs with no time wasted.

ironDev Community

FusionUI is completely inner-source, and we hope to see fellow ironSourcerers contribute to the project. Our plan is to see FusionUI used beyond the walls of ironSource in the future.

Power of Diversity

They say 2 minds are better than 1. Well, 365 R&D minds are definitely better than 2. ironSource developers have a variety of skill sets, so let’s put our diversity to use.

The Technology Stack

FusionUI makes front-end web development faster and easier. Organize your UI layer alongside your platform logic with FusionUI. It’s made for all skill levels, devices of all shapes, and projects of all sizes.
